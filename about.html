About Us

Praying, discipling, and serving with open hands.

Our Calling

Emmanuel Evangelical Mission exists to worship Jesus Christ, strengthen believers through Scripture, and serve communities with compassion. Our prayer is simple and steady: may North India know the peace, truth, and love of God.

Our Commitments

  • Christ-centered worship and Biblical teaching
  • Prayer for families, villages, cities, and leaders
  • Care for the poor, lonely, and hurting
  • Raising disciples who serve with humility
